06 — Voice & Tone

Voice & Tone

geo-tool speaks like a knowledgeable colleague — direct, confident, without buzzword soup.

✓ Do
"Your brand is missing from 3 AI search engines. Here's why — and how to fix it in 48 hours."
Direct problem → direct solution. Confidence without arrogance.
✗ Don't
"Leveraging AI-powered synergies to holistically optimize your digital footprint across next-gen generative ecosystems."
Buzzword overload. Zero substance.
✓ Do
"GEO Score: 94. You're in the top 5% of tracked brands. ChatGPT cites you 3× more than your closest competitor."
Numbers, context, comparison. Data builds trust.
✗ Don't
"Unlock the potential of your brand's AI presence journey with our revolutionary platform solution."
Vague aspirational language. Says nothing, promises everything.
Tone Attributes
⚡
Direct
Lead with the insight. Short sentences. Active voice.
🔬
Precise
Specific numbers over vague claims. Cite sources. Show methodology.
🤝
Human
We work with you, not at you. No jargon without explanation.
